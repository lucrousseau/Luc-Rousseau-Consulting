- Related articles section at end of each post

## 📝 Content Guidelines

### Tone & Positioning

- **Premium positioning:** No generic freelancer language
- **Strategic focus:** Business impact, not just technical skills
- **Credible for CTO/CEO audience:** Concise, sharp, authoritative
- **No "cheap marketing":** Avoid hype, focus on value

### SEO Best Practices

- **Keyword density:** 1-2% (natural integration)
- **H1:** One per page, includes primary keyword
- **H2/H3:** Include secondary keywords naturally
- **Internal links:** 3-5 per article
- **Word count:** 1500-2500 words for authority posts
- **Images:** All with descriptive alt text including keywords where appropriate

### Content Structure

- **Intro:** Hook + problem statement (2-3 paragraphs)
- **Body:** Clear H2/H3 structure, actionable insights
- **Conclusion:** Summary + CTA
- **CTA:** Always link to relevant service/contact page
